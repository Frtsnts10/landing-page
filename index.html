Who We Are

Established for Excellence

PT. Vysco Indo Pratama is a General Contractor established in 2003. We have built a reputation for strength and reliability in agro-industry construction, civil infrastructure, and industrial projects.

Our core values define every project we undertake: Total Responsibility, Trust, Comfort, and Environment.
